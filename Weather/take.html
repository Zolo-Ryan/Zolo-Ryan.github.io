Weather Measures

Rainy Weather

When it's raining, remember to carry an umbrella and wear waterproof clothing to stay dry.

Sunny Weather

On a sunny day, don't forget to apply sunscreen, wear a hat, and stay hydrated.

Snowy Weather

During snowy weather, wear warm clothing, boots, and be cautious of slippery surfaces.

Windy Weather

When it's windy, secure loose objects, dress in layers, and protect your eyes from blowing debris.

Foggy Weather

In foggy conditions, drive with caution, use low-beam headlights, and allow extra time for travel.
